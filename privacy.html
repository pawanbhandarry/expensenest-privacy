Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

ExpenseNest respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the ExpenseNest mobile application.

1. Information Collection

ExpenseNest does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data you enter (expenses, income, projects, receipts) is stored locally on your device only.

2. No Account Required

ExpenseNest does not require account creation, email registration, or login.

3. Offline Usage

The app works fully offline. Your financial information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. Camera & Storage Permissions

ExpenseNest may request access to:

  • Camera – for scanning receipts (optional)
  • Storage – for saving receipt images and exporting files

These permissions are used only for app functionality and never for data collection.

5. Data Sharing

ExpenseNest does NOT share any data with third parties.

6. Analytics & Tracking

ExpenseNest does not use analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising networks, or cloud services.

7. Data Backup

You may manually export your data. These files remain under your control. ExpenseNest does not upload backups anywhere.

8. Security

All information remains on your device. You may enable biometric or PIN protection if supported by your phone.

9. Children’s Privacy

ExpenseNest does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
